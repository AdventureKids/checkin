Common Questions

What hardware do I need?

ChurchCheck runs in any web browser on tablets, laptops, or touch-screen computers. For label printing, we support Dymo LabelWriter printers (450 series recommended).

Can I import my existing data?

Yes! Our smart import tool accepts CSV files and helps you map columns to our database fields. Most churches are up and running in under an hour.

Does it work offline?

ChurchCheck includes a desktop app for Mac and Windows that works even without internet. Data syncs automatically when you're back online.

How do the attendance incentives work?

Kids earn attendance streaks (consecutive weeks), milestone badges (Bronze at 4 weeks, Silver at 8, Gold at 12), and can earn rewards you customize. It's all automatic ‚Äî no extra work for volunteers.

Is our data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted, we never sell your information, and you can export or delete your data anytime. We take child safety and privacy seriously.

Can we turn off the incentives?

Yes! Every incentive feature can be toggled on or off. Use ChurchCheck as a simple, secure check-in system ‚Äî or enable the full rewards experience. Your choice.
